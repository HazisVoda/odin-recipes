Description

Ingredients

  • Flat iron steak
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Season the steak: Rub the steak with olive oil, garlic, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sear the steak: Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat. Add the steak and sear for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  3. Finish the steak: Add butter to the skillet and baste the steak with the melted butter. Remove the steak from the skillet and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
